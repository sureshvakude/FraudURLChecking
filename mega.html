Welcome to our site

Our website is a fraud URL detection service that helps protect you from online scams and phishing attacks. With our website, you can quickly and easily check the legitimacy of any website.

How It Works

Simply enter the URL you want to check into the search bar, and our website will run a comprehensive check to determine whether the site is safe or not. If the site is deemed unsafe, our site will alert you and provide recommendations on how to protect yourself.

Features

Comprehensive Checks

Our tool performs comprehensive checks on URLs, including checks for phishing scams, malware, and other types of fraud. This ensures that you can use our tool to protect yourself from a wide range of online threats.

Real-time Analysis

Our tool provides real-time analysis of URLs, which means that you can get up-to-date information about the safety of a website before you visit it. This helps you make informed decisions and stay safe online.

User-friendly Interface

We've designed our tool with a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for you to check URLs and get information about their safety. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced user, our tool is simple to use and navigate.

Multi-platform Compatibility

Our tool is compatible with multiple platforms, including desktop and mobile devices. This means that you can use our tool on your preferred device and check URLs on the go.
